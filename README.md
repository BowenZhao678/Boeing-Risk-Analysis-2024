# Financial Risk Analysis of Boeing’s 2024 Safety Incidents

This project analyzes major Boeing aircraft safety incidents in 2024 and their impact on operational and financial risk.

## Project Overview
The project examines:
- major Boeing incidents in 2024
- financial effects on cash flow, earnings, and delivery schedules
- operational risk, regulatory risk, and reputational risk
- the role of engineering quality and corporate culture in long-term risk management

## Files
- `Boeing Financial Risk Report.pdf` – full written report
- `Boeing Presentation.pdf` – presentation slides

## Key Takeaway
The project shows that long-term risk management depends not only on financial results, but also on engineering quality, product safety, and corporate culture.
